OUR MISSION

We are committed to informing everyday people about the effects of metal waste and encouraging them to recycle.

It is important to spread the word about recycling and the consequences about not doing so. Not recycling can cause damage to the environment, as it creates the need to mine for more metal and causes pollution and damage to wildlife.

We believe the best way to reduce the negative effects of metal mining is by reducing metal waste — and that the best way to reduce metal waste is to educate the public and encourage them to recycle.

Environmentally Friendly

Environmental damage is something we think should be taken very seriously. We believe that ecosystems should not be compromised for the benefit of humans. We strive to find low impact solutions and practices for damaging industries like metal ore mining.

Sustainable

We believe industries should operate in a sustainable manner. This includes improving industries, especially those that operate on the extraction of limited natural resources like the metal ore mining industry.
