HOW CAN I HELP?

The best thing you can do is recycle. Keep a separate bin in your house for metals and plastics, and, when they are full, you can take them to a local recycling facility. Some states even pay you for your contribution to the environment. Recycling helps protect the environment in many ways and keeps valuable metals in our economy, reducing the need to mine for more.
